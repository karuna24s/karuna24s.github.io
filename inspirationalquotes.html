Quote from: Dear Zindagi (Dear Life)

When we felt like crying in our childhood,

elders used to tell us to wipe our tears.

When we used to get angry,

elders used to say that we should smile.

We weren’t given the permission to hate someone.

And when we tried to love someone,

we came to know that this emotional system

has some problem, it cannot function.

When we are not allowed to express grief,

anger or hatred openly,

then how can we express love?



Quotes from: Rang De Basanti (The Color of Sacrifice)

In India, the color yellow represents sacrifice for a noble/good cause.

To live life there are only two ways ...

keep tolerating it ...
